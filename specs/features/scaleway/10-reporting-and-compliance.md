# Scaleway Stories - Feature 10 Reporting and Compliance

Feature source: [10-reporting-and-compliance.md](/Users/kees/data/projects/archive/features/10-reporting-and-compliance.md)

## Scaleway components

- Managed PostgreSQL
- OpenSearch
- Audit Trail
- Cockpit
- Serverless Containers

## Stories

- `SCW-10-01` Retention reporting by status, upcoming expiry and lifecycle tier.
- `SCW-10-02` Legal hold reporting on active holds and affected document sets.
- `SCW-10-03` Audit reporting by actor, period and document scope.
- `SCW-10-04` Evidence package generation with metadata, checksum, policy and audit context.
- `SCW-10-05` Compliance dashboards for backlog, cold storage footprint and restore activity.

## Acceptance focus

- compliance data is reproducible from source systems
- lifecycle and hold posture are visible without manual joining
- evidence packages are auditor-friendly
